Upcoming Facilities

New Arts College Building

We are excited to announce that construction of a new Arts college building is currently in progress. This modern facility will include:

  • Spacious and well-equipped classrooms
  • Modern library with expanded collection
  • Computer lab for digital learning
  • Seminar hall for events and presentations
  • Staff rooms and administrative offices
  • Enhanced sanitation facilities

Proposed Science College

Following the community's proposal, we are planning to establish a Science college that will feature:

  • State-of-the-art science laboratories
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Biology labs
  • Computer science facilities
  • Modern equipment and apparatus
  • Dedicated science library
