Our Story

Madhyamik Vidyalay Kalmadu was established in 2008 with a vision to provide quality education to the children of Kalmadu village and surrounding areas. What started as a secondary school has now grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving students from grade 8 to grade 12.

Our journey has been marked by continuous growth and development. We began with secondary education (grades 8-10) and have now expanded to include Arts stream for higher secondary education (grades 11-12). With a new Arts college building currently under construction and plans for a Science college, we are committed to providing diverse educational opportunities to our community.

Located in the heart of Kalmadu village, our institution is easily accessible and serves as a beacon of hope and progress for rural education in Maharashtra.
